## 3D Cake Model Details

### Visual Appearance
```
        🍒 (Cherry on top)
        │
    ┌───┴───┐
    │ ◉ ◉ ◉ │  ← Top tier (smallest)
    └───────┘     Pink with gold decorations
    
    ┌─────────┐
    │ ◉ ◉ ◉ ◉ │  ← Middle tier
    └─────────┘     Light pink with gold
    
    ┌───────────┐
    │ ◉ ◉ ◉ ◉ ◉ │  ← Bottom tier (largest)
    └───────────┘     Very light pink with gold
    
    ═══════════     ← Shadow on ground
```

### Lighting Setup
- **Ambient Light:** Soft overall illumination
- **Spot Light:** From top-right, creates highlights
- **Point Lights:** Pink and purple accent lights
- **Environment:** Sunset preset for warm glow

### Animation
- **Rotation:** Continuous 360° rotation (1 rev/sec)
- **Floating:** Gentle up-down motion (sine wave)
- **Interactive:** User can drag to rotate manually
